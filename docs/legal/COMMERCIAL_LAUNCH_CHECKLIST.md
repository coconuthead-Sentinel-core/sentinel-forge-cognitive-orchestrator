# Commercial Launch Checklist

## Current Status
The Sentinel Forge repository is complete for engineering and hiring review, but it is **not** currently operating as a public commercial SaaS service.

## What Is True Today
- No public payment flow is active from this repository
- No merchant processor is configured through this repository release
- No customer-facing hosted production service is being advertised as live
- The active release is a review-ready repository package on `main`

## What Would Be Required Before Commercial Launch
- Legal business entity and tax registration chosen by the owner
- Public hosting and operations workflow established
- Published privacy policy and terms aligned to the hosted service
- Refund and cancellation terms aligned to the actual billing model
- Merchant onboarding completed
- Customer support and incident contacts established

## Review Decision
Commercial launch is outside the current work cycle and is not a blocker for technical review, HR review, or portfolio review of this repository.
