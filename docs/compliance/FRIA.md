## 3. Fundamental Rights Assessment

| Rights Area | Risk | Likelihood | Severity | Mitigation | Residual |
|------------|------|-----------|---------|------------|---------|
| Human Dignity (Art. 1) | Outputs demean users | Low | High | Advisory only; content policy active | 🟢 LOW |
| Privacy (Arts. 7 & 8) | Voice data captured unexpectedly | Medium | High | No persistent voice storage; session-only | 🟡 MEDIUM |
| Privacy (Arts. 7 & 8) | Sentinel Profile treated as personal data | Low | Medium | Profile stores preferences, not identity | 🟢 LOW |
| Non-Discrimination (Art. 21) | Lens outputs stigmatize cognitive styles | Low | High | Strength-based design; evaluation benchmarked | 🟢 LOW |
| Freedom of Expression (Art. 11) | System suppresses or distorts user voice | Very Low | Low | System amplifies — does not censor | 🟢 VERY LOW |
| Effective Remedy (Art. 47) | No recourse if voice AI causes harm | Medium | Medium | Incident log; mock mode available | 🟡 LOW-MEDIUM |

**Overall FRIA Result: LOW RISK** — The voice interface introduces a minor elevated risk around audio privacy, addressed by session-only processing and no persistent storage.
